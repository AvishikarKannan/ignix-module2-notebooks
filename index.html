About the Workshop

Join us for an interactive data science workshop where you'll dive deep into Python's most powerful libraries. IGNIX Module 2 is designed to give you hands-on experience with NumPy for numerical computing, Pandas for data manipulation, and Matplotlib for data visualization. Whether you're a beginner or looking to sharpen your skills, this workshop will provide practical knowledge you can apply immediately.
